<p><p>Agave syrup, derived from the agave plant, is available primarily in two types: light and dark. Light agave syrup has a mild flavor and a lighter color, making it ideal for sweetening beverages and desserts without overpowering other tastes. Dark agave syrup, on the other hand, has a more robust flavor and caramel color, lending depth to recipes such as marinades and baked goods. Both types are popular as natural sweeteners among health-conscious consumers seeking alternatives to refined sugars.</p></p>
