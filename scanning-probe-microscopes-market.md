<p><p>The scanning probe microscopes market includes Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM), Scanning Tunneling Microscopes (STM), and Near-field Scanning Optical Microscopes (NSOM). AFM uses a cantilever with a sharp tip to measure surface forces, enabling high-resolution imaging of materials. STM utilizes quantum tunneling to analyze conductive surfaces at the atomic level. NSOM combines optical and scanning probe techniques, allowing imaging beyond the diffraction limit. Each type serves distinct applications across nanotechnology, materials science, and biomedical research, driving market growth.</p></p>
