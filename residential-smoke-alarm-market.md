<p><strong>The Residential Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smoke alarm market includes three primary types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms use a light source to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization alarms are designed to sense flaming fires more quickly by using radioactive material to ionize the air. Combination alarms integrate both technologies, providing comprehensive fire detection by responding to a wider range of fire types, thus enhancing safety in residential settings.</p></p>
