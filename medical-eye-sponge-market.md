<p><p>The Medical Eye Sponge market is segmented into PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) and Cellulose types. PVA sponges are highly absorbent, providing effective fluid management during ocular procedures due to their softness and ability to maintain structural integrity when wet. In contrast, Cellulose sponges, made from natural fibers, are biodegradable and offer a different absorbency profile, often preferred for their environmental friendliness and gentler texture. Both types are essential in ophthalmic surgeries, catering to specific procedural needs and preferences.</p></p>
