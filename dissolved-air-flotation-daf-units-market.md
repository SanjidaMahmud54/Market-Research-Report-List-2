<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Units are a water treatment technology used primarily for solid-liquid separation. This method involves dissolving air in water under pressure and then releasing the pressure, causing the air to form micro-bubbles. These micro-bubbles attach to suspended solids, making them buoyant and facilitating their rise to the surface for removal. DAF units are widely used in industries such as municipal wastewater treatment, food and beverage processing, and chemical manufacturing due to their efficiency in handling high solids loads and improving water clarity.</p><p>The DAF Units Market is on a growth trajectory, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for effective wastewater management solutions, stringent environmental regulations, and the rising need for advanced water treatment technologies.
