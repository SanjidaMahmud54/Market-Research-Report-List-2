<p><p>The digital medical gas alarm market encompasses three primary types: Master Alarms, Area Alarms, and Local Alarms. Master Alarms serve as centralized monitoring systems that provide an overview of all gas supply conditions in a facility, ensuring compliance and safety. Area Alarms indicate alarm status specific to designated zones, helping to identify issues within particular sections. Local Alarms are individual alerts at the point of use, offering immediate notification to staff about gas supply malfunctions, enhancing patient safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
