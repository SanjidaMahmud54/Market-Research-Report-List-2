<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Turntables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-drive Turntable</li><li>Belt-drive Turntable</li><li>Idler-wheel Turntable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turntable market is categorized into three main types: direct-drive, belt-drive, and idler-wheel turntables. Direct-drive turntables feature a motor directly beneath the platter, offering quick start-up and reliable speed stability, making them popular for DJs. Belt-drive turntables use a rubber or silicone belt to connect the motor and platter, resulting in reduced vibration and a warmer sound, ideal for audiophiles. Idler-wheel turntables utilize a rubber wheel to transfer power, combining elements of both designs, preferred for vintage enthusiasts seeking unique sound characteristics.</p></p>
