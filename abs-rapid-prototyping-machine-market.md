<p><p>The ABS Rapid Prototyping Machine market encompasses different technologies, primarily Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Material Extrusion Modeling (MEM). FDM technology utilizes a continuous filament of thermoplastic material, layer by layer, to create prototypes, making it suitable for producing durable, functional parts. MEM, on the other hand, works by extruding materials in a more varied manner, allowing for diverse material properties and rapid production of complex geometries. Both technologies cater to industries seeking efficient prototyping solutions.</p></p>
