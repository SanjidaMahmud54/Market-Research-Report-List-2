<p><p>The waste tire cracking equipment market comprises two main types: continuous and intermittent. Continuous type systems operate non-stop, processing tires in a steady flow and ideal for high-volume operations, enhancing efficiency and reducing downtime. In contrast, intermittent type systems process tires in batches, allowing for flexible production scales and adjustments based on demand. Both systems cater to different operational needs, with continuous types favored for large-scale recycling facilities, while intermittent types are suitable for smaller enterprises or specialized applications.</p></p>
