<p><p>High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) lipoplasty devices are classified into two main types: ultrasound-guided and MRI-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target fat deposits, allowing for immediate feedback during the procedure. In contrast, MRI-guided HIFU employs magnetic resonance imaging for enhanced visualization, offering greater accuracy and safety by providing detailed anatomical information. Both technologies aim to improve non-invasive body contouring by selectively destroying fat cells while preserving surrounding tissues.</p></p>
