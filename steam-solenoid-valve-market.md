<p><p>The steam solenoid valve market encompasses two main types: saturated steam solenoid valves and superheated steam solenoid valves. Saturated steam valves are designed to handle steam at its boiling point, ensuring efficient control in applications like heating and sterilization. In contrast, superheated steam valves manage steam at temperatures above its boiling point, facilitating higher efficiency in industrial processes such as power generation and chemical production. Both types play vital roles in automated systems requiring precise steam management.</p></p>
