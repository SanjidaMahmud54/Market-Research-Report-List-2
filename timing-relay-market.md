<p><strong>The Timing Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-delay</li><li>Off-delay</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The timing relay market consists of several types, primarily categorized into on-delay, off-delay, and other relays. On-delay relays activate an output after a predetermined time once the input signal is received, ideal for applications requiring a start-up timeout. Off-delay relays, conversely, maintain the output for a set duration after the input signal is lost, useful for controlled shutdowns. Other types may include multifunction and repeat cycle relays, addressing specialized needs across diverse industrial and automation sectors.</p></p>
