<p><p>The EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance) anti-theft antenna market consists of three primary types: Radio Frequency (RF), Acousto-Magnetic (AM), and Electromagnetic (EM) systems. RF systems use radio waves to detect tags attached to stolen items, while AM systems rely on a combination of acoustic and magnetic signatures for detection. EM systems utilize electromagnetic fields to identify tagged products. Each type offers varying levels of detection efficiency, cost, and installation requirements, catering to different retail environments and security needs.</p></p>
