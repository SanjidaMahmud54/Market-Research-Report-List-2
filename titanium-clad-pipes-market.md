<p><strong>The Titanium Clad Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roll Bonding Process</li><li>Mechanically</li><li>Explosive Bonding Process</li><li>Weld Overlay Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium clad pipes are produced using various bonding processes to enhance the properties of traditional piping materials. The roll bonding process involves mechanically pressing titanium onto a substrate, creating a strong bond. Mechanically bonded pipes use fasteners or other physical methods for attachment. The explosive bonding process utilizes controlled explosions to fuse titanium with underlying materials, ensuring high bond strength. The weld overlay process involves depositing a layer of titanium onto pipes through welding techniques, providing corrosion resistance and durability. Each method caters to specific application needs.</p></p>
