<p><p>The Invasive Fetal Monitoring market encompasses several key technologies used to assess fetal well-being during pregnancy and labor. Ultrasound and ultrasonography provide real-time imaging, while electronic fetal monitoring (EFM) continuously tracks fetal heart rates. Fetal electrodes measure electrical activity directly from the fetus, and fetal Doppler devices utilize sound waves to monitor heart rates non-invasively. Uterine contraction monitors assess uterine activity, helping manage labor. Other devices may include telemetry systems and advanced diagnostic tools that enhance fetal health monitoring.</p></p>
