<p><strong>The Pylon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tension Tower</li><li>Straight Tower</li><li>Corner Tower</li><li>Transposition Tower</li><li>Terminal Tower</li><li>Spanning Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pylon market types include various structures used in power transmission line systems. Tension towers manage cable tension, while straight towers maintain linear alignment. Corner towers handle directional changes, and transposition towers allow for cable swapping to balance electrical load. Terminal towers mark the beginning or end of transmission lines, while spanning towers support conductors over long distances without intermediate support. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring efficient and safe power distribution across diverse landscapes.</p></p>
