<p><p>The dash cam market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel dash cameras. Single-channel dash cameras capture video from the front of the vehicle, providing essential evidence in case of accidents. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras offer footage from multiple angles, typically including both front and rear views, enhancing overall surveillance and safety. Multi-channel systems are ideal for those seeking comprehensive coverage, while single-channel models cater to users prioritizing simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
