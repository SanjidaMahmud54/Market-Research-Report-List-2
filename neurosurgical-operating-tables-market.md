<p><p>The neurosurgical operating tables market includes three primary types: electric, hydraulic, and manual operating tables. Electric operating tables offer precise positioning and adjustments through motorized controls, enhancing surgical efficiency and comfort. Hydraulic operating tables utilize fluid power for elevation and tilt, providing stability and flexibility during procedures. Manual operating tables require manual adjustment for positioning, offering a cost-effective solution but less convenience. Each type caters to varying surgical needs, budget considerations, and hospital requirements in the neurosurgery field.</p></p>
