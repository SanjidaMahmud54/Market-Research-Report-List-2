<p><p>Metal impregnation plants are classified into two main market types: manual operation and fully automatic systems. Manual operation plants rely on human intervention for processes such as handling, monitoring, and controlling impregnating agents, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations or specific applications. In contrast, fully automatic plants utilize advanced automation technologies for streamlined processes, enhancing efficiency, precision, and productivity. These systems reduce labor costs and minimize human error, catering to larger-scale industrial needs and demanding production environments.</p></p>
