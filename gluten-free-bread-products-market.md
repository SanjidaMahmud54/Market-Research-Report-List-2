<p><p>The gluten-free bread products market is primarily divided into two types: no-gluten bread and little-gluten bread. No-gluten bread is entirely free from gluten, making it suitable for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance. In contrast, little-gluten bread contains minimal gluten levels, catering to those with mild sensitivities who can tolerate trace amounts. Both categories have gained popularity, driven by increasing awareness of gluten-related disorders and a growing demand for alternative dietary options.</p></p>
