<p><p>The ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) conductive glass market can be categorized into high resistance, low resistance, and normal glass types. High resistance glass has a high sheet resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring minimal conductivity, such as specialized sensors. Low resistance glass offers better conductivity, ideal for touch screens and displays, ensuring efficient signal transmission. Normal glass provides standard conductivity levels, typically used in various general applications. Each type caters to specific needs in electronic and display technologies.</p></p>
