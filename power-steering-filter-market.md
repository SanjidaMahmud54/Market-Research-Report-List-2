<p><strong>The Power Steering Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fit Filter</li><li>In-Line Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power steering filter market primarily consists of two types: direct fit filters and in-line filters. Direct fit filters are designed to seamlessly replace the original equipment in a vehicle's power steering system, ensuring compatibility and ease of installation. In-line filters, on the other hand, are installed within the power steering lines, providing additional filtration and protection from contaminants. Both types enhance the efficiency and longevity of the power steering system, catering to various consumer preferences and vehicle models.</p></p>
