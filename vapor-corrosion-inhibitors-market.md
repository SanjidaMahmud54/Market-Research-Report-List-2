<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vapor Corrosion Inhibitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anode Inhibitor</li><li>Cathode Inhibitor</li><li>Mixed Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapor Corrosion Inhibitors (VCIs) are classified into three types based on their protective mechanisms. Anode inhibitors protect the anode part of metals by forming a barrier that reduces oxidation. Cathode inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent corrosion at the cathode, enhancing the protective layer and slowing down electron transfer. Mixed inhibitors serve to protect both anode and cathode surfaces by providing comprehensive corrosion resistance, making them versatile for various applications in the packaging and preservation of metal goods.</p></p>
