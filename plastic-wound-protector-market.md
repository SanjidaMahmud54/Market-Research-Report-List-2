<p><strong>The Plastic Wound Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ring Based</li><li>Prong Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plastic Wound Protector Market can be segmented into two primary types: Ring-Based and Prong-Based protectors. Ring-Based wound protectors consist of a circular structure that creates a stable barrier around the incision site, facilitating a clear view and preventing contamination. In contrast, Prong-Based wound protectors utilize multiple protruding prongs to secure the protector in place, allowing for more flexibility and adaptability during surgical procedures. Each type serves to enhance surgical visibility and protect the wound from external contaminants.</p></p>
