<p><p>The Prepaid Electricity Sales Management System comprises three market types. IC Card Electricity Sales involve consumers purchasing preloaded cards that can be used at designated kiosks for electricity credits. Remote Electricity Sales utilize online platforms or apps, enabling customers to buy electricity remotely without physical interaction. Mixed Electricity Sales combine both methods, offering flexibility for consumers to choose between IC cards and online purchases. This diverse setup enhances accessibility and convenience for consumers in managing their electricity usage and payments.</p></p>
