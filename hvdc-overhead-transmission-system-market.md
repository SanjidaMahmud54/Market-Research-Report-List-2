<p><strong>The HVDC Overhead Transmission System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point-to-Point Transmission</li><li>Back-to-Back Station</li><li>Multi-Terminal System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC overhead transmission system market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Point-to-Point Transmission** connects two substations, efficiently transmitting electricity over long distances and minimizing losses.</p><p>2. **Back-to-Back Station** facilitates the interconnection of two asynchronous power systems, allowing for frequency and voltage stabilization between them.</p><p>3. **Multi-Terminal System** enables power exchange among multiple locations, enhancing grid reliability and flexibility by integrating various generation sources and facilitating increased renewable energy use. These systems contribute significantly to modern electric transmission infrastructure.</p></p>
