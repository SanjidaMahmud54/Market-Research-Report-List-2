<p><p>The forklift lead-acid battery market comprises three main types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, making them suitable for indoor environments with limited ventilation. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and are typically more economical for high-capacity applications. Other types may include advanced lead-acid variants and specialty batteries designed for specific operational needs. Each type serves distinct operational requirements in industrial and material handling applications.</p></p>
