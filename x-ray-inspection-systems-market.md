<p><ul><li>Digital Radiography (DR) Type</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray inspection systems encompass various technologies, including Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes flat-panel detectors to capture high-resolution images in real time, allowing for quicker inspections and better image quality. In contrast, CT combines multiple X-ray images taken from different angles to produce detailed cross-sectional images of objects. Both technologies are crucial in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare, aiding in defect detection, quality assurance, and internal structure analysis.</p></p>
