<p><p>The flexible electronic skin patch market encompasses various sensor types designed for health monitoring and diagnostics. Biochemical sensors analyze bodily fluids for biomarkers, aiding in disease detection. Potentiometric sensors detect changes in chemical concentrations, useful for monitoring pH or ion levels.
