The increasing prevalence of orthopedic surgeries, surgeries involving limb procedures, and a growing emphasis on effective post-operative pain management are driving demand for CPNB catheters.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates a robust expansion in the CPNB catheter market, anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 11.10% during the forecast period.
