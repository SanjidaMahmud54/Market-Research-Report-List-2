<p><p>The implantable ventricular assist devices (VAD) market includes three main types: left ventricular assist devices (LVADs), right ventricular assist devices (RVADs), and biventricular assist devices (BIVADs). LVADs primarily support the left ventricle, essential for patients with heart failure. RVADs assist the right ventricle, while BIVADs provide support to both ventricles simultaneously. Each type addresses specific cardiac dysfunctions, catering to patients with varying degrees of heart failure, thereby enhancing survival rates and quality of life.</p></p>
