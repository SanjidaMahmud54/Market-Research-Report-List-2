<p><ul><li>Electrostatic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical antibacterial filter market primarily includes two types: electrostatic and mechanical filters. Electrostatic filters utilize an electric charge to attract and trap airborne pathogens and particles, enhancing their efficiency in capturing small particles. On the other hand, mechanical filters rely on physical barriers, such as various materials and mesh sizes, to physically block contaminants from passing through. Both types play a critical role in maintaining sterile environments in medical settings, thereby reducing the risk of infections and improving patient safety.</p></p>
